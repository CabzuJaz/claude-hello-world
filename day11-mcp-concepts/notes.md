# Day 11 — MCP Concepts
**Date:** May 2, 2026

---

## What is MCP?
**Model Context Protocol** — an open-source standard for connecting AI applications to external systems.

Instead of hardcoding tools into every script, MCP gives AI models a **standardized, bidirectional way** to talk to:

| Type | Examples |
|------|---------|
| **Data Sources** | Local files, databases, Notion, Google Calendar |
| **Tools** | Search engines, calculators, APIs |
| **Workflows** | Specialized prompt templates |

---

## What Can MCP Enable?
- Agents that access Google Calendar and Notion → more personalized assistants
- Claude Code generating entire web apps from a Figma design
- Chatbots querying multiple databases across an organization via chat
- AI models creating 3D designs in Blender and sending them to a 3D printer

---

## Why Does MCP Matter?

| Who | Benefit |
|-----|---------|
| **Developers** | Reduces development time and complexity when building or integrating AI apps |
| **AI Apps / Agents** | Access to an ecosystem of data sources, tools, and apps — enhanced capabilities |
| **End Users** | More capable AI that can access your data and take actions on your behalf |
